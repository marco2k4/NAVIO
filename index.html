Technology Roadmap

Three Phases,
One Mission

From QR codes to full AR vision — a progressive upgrade path built for reliability.

Phase 01 · Months 1–3
📍
QR Navigation
200 QR codes at doors, stairwells, and corners. Scan to get instant turn-by-turn directions.
±10m accuracy
Phase 02 · Months 4–6
📡
Bluetooth Beacons
100–150 BLE beacons broadcast continuously. Auto-detects your position — no scanning needed.
±3m accuracy
Phase 03 · Months 7–8
🥽
AR Vision
3D blue arrows float on your camera feed. The first AR campus navigation in Canada.
±1m accuracy
